    Welcome! We are open to all.

    St. Joseph's Mission Hospital - Migori is a multi-specialty, health care hospital with a current capacity of 200 fully functional operational beds.

    It provides quality out-patient and in-patient facilities with dedicated wards, specialty clinics, theatre, and laboratory.

    We consistently strive to be a modern centre of excellence in provision of quality and affordable health care for all according to the Catholic faith.
